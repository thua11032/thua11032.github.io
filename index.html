Experience

Over the years, I managed to work at some really cool places on some really cool projects! Take a look at some of my past jobs and projects below! Click them to learn more!

NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory

Origami inspired deployable solar arrays and a capture and sterilization system for a Mars Sample Return Mission, my internship experiences at JPL have been spectacular! For both projects, I was able to fully take control of the project from start to finish: design, prototype, test, design reviews, re-design, re-pitch, the list goes on. I definitely had fun and learned alot.

OceanLab Autonomous Underwater Swarm Robotics

At a small startup located in Glendale, CA, I worked together with the team to develop new vehicles and swarming algorithms to further robotic swarm exploration of the ocean! My awesome summer experience allowed me to learn so much more about GPS locking, electronics breadboarding, arduino/python programming, environmental testing, and more! Amongst my contributions to the company's first product, I also designed and deployed my own floating water current and weather collection station!

National Solar Boat Competition Winner

"Why does that highschool have a boat and we don't!?!" That question led me to start my school's engineering club, which has since evolved into a full fledge engineering program with classes, guest speakers, field trips, and afterschool projects. From pieces of plywood, rolls of cables, sheets of mono-silicon solar cells, and 7 months of fundraising and building, my small team of highschool students designed and created the solar powered boat that won 1st place in our maiden year of competition.

Skills and Hobbies

In addition to being a student at school studying or interning at companies, I also enjoy various other activities in my free time. My skills and hobbies are collected down below!

Mechanical Design/ Prototyping

Mechanical design is my speciality! Ask me to draft a part or do stress analysis for another and I will deliver! Some of the software packages that I use include: SolidWorks, NX Unigraphics, Autodesk Inventor, AutoCAD, AutoCAD Electrical, Revit, ProE/Creo Parametric, Coventor, CADSoft EAGLE PCB design, Pepakura Designer, Google SketchUp, and more! I am also learning many new ones including Catia, Maya, Blender, and Rhinoceros.

In addition to computer modelling, I am also well versed in prototyping/manufacturing processes. I can use CNC machines, saws, lathes, drills, sanders and more to fashion wood. I can cut, bend, and weld metal and plastics. I am also superb at 3D printing and laser cutting. I've done composite layering and forming, worked with surface protectants, and more. The list is endless and ever growing!

Electronic Design / Repairs/ Debugging

Since middle school, I have also hacked and worked with electronics of all kinds. From broken television sets to old desktop computers, I've taken them apart and fixed them up again, even those covered in rust. I build high end gaming computers for clients and now work as a tech consultant, network manager, and educator for Stanford's academic computing department.

Delving deeper in electronics, I also understand PCB design and etching. I am proficient in probing connections and debugging circuits, soldering and splicing connections together when necessary.

Software Design / Web Development

Autonomous robots, autonomous cars, autonomous machine learning; the same could be said about my drive to learn software! I am a fast and driven learner when it comes to software and can easily pick up languages and methods as I go. My past experiences in JAVA, C/C++, Python, Perl, Unix, Arduino, Android, HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, Matlab, etc have given me enough knowledge to make my own videogame, an automated testbed for students, multiplexing music reactive LED lightshows, an electro-cardiogram, and even this website! The idea of IOT amazes me and I want nothing more than to help contribute to the growing field that will be the future of our society.

Graphic Design

From large New Year's Festivals to clinics and small businesses, I have performed freelance logo and flyer designs for various clients. I've also managed Facebook marketing and advertising for numerous events and spearheaded event promotions from physical brochures, flyers, and pamphlets to digital media including flyers and promotional videos. With a professional look and feel, I can assure you more customers and traffic to whatever occasion you are hosting and I am the guy for that!
