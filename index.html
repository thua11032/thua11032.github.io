About Me

Hello there! I am currently a mechanical engineering student at Stanford University. I love designing and fabricating mechanisms for consumer electronics, vehicles, sustainable technolgies, and robots! I am also very interested in smart and autonomous technologies if a job opportunity in that sector ever arises! To see more of my engineering background, please follow the linkedin link below to see if my engineering experience resonates with your needs!

In my spare time, I enjoy pursuing creative content in the form of online graphic art, film, photography, or even paper origami folding. I love tackling complex origami shapes and have made a few on commision basis. I regularly produce flyers and branding for different organizations and small businesses, as well as logo creation for a large hospital chain in the past. When not being paid, I still enjoy producing content for the fun of it and would hike miles for the perfect picture when I get the chance!

Some of the past companies and clients I've worked with includes NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Apium Swarm Robotics, Samtec Optical Group, Sutter Health hospital group, Stanford's outreach office, and that's just the tip of the iceburg!
