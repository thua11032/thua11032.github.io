Humble beginings, not so humble aspirations

Hi, I'm Tony Hua!

I am a mechanical engineering graduate from Stanford University, currently a powerpack installation lead at BAE Systems, owner of 2 small (but highly automated!) ecommerce stores, a freelance designer, and an event photographer and nature enthusiast on weekends!

I am extremely curious by nature and fortunately, I am also resourceful and proactive about diging deeper to learn everything I can about about topics I am interested in. I take pride in all my random projects and skills, from building cardboard-based origami furniture to programming automated 3D printer assembly lines.

I love exploring new technologies and am always on the lookout for new opportunities to collaborate and learn! To name a few, NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Samtec Semicondutor, Apium Swarm Robotics, and TESLA are just a few of my past clients and employers. If you have a cool project in mind, please do not hesitate to contact me!
